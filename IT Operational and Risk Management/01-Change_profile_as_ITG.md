# IBM OpenPages IT GRC — Initial Navigation Tutorial  
### Steps by steos — Accessing the Settings Menu & Changing Profiles

This short guide walks you through the first steps when navigating IBM OpenPages for IT GRC.  

Access the Link for open pages below:

[Openpages_url](http://jp-tok.services.cloud.techzone.ibm.com:22650/openpages/)

---

# **Step 1 — Open the Settings Menu**
![Step 1](https://github.com/Client-Engineering-Indonesia/workshop-openpages/blob/main/IT%20Operational%20and%20Risk%20Management/others/0.1.%20press%20the%20gear%20button%20and%20choose%20change%20profile.png)

In the upper-right corner of the OpenPages interface, click the **Person icon**.  
This icon opens the system settings panel where you can access configuration options.

**Why this step matters:**  
You need to open the settings menu before you can change your working profile or adjust any system preferences.

---

# **Step 2 — Change Your Active Profile**
![Step 2](https://github.com/Client-Engineering-Indonesia/workshop-openpages/blob/main/IT%20Operational%20and%20Risk%20Management/others/0.2%20change%20profile%20to%20OpenPages%20ITG%207.2.0%20Master.png)

After clicking the gear icon, a settings menu will appear.  
Select **“Change Profile”** and choose the profile name:

```text
OpenPages ITG 7.2.0 Master
```
