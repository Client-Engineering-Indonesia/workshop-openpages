# Define Risk and Control

## Identify Process Risk

1. In your new sub process page, click Select Risk button -> type ```P01-RSK-01-01``` in Name search field -> select risk with High residual risk rating -> click Done button

<img width="1720" height="690" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/d08d7a20-9361-40d4-be9b-86ae6b68486e" />

2. As you can see, new risk has been associated to your sub process

<img width="1376" height="299" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/505dee1e-ae21-4367-b640-85ba7372f57e" />

3. Click new associated risk -> scroll down until you find Related Content section -> you can see list of controls for this risk

<img width="1387" height="499" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/72400386-1769-41e0-a83c-3034830bbb40" />

## Review Parent Process

Go back to your Process page and you can see that there is change visual matrix on section Inherent Risk Rating which is number 1 on quadrant Medium-Medium. This happens because risk P01-RSK-01-01 has Medium Inherent impact and Medium Inherent likelihood.
